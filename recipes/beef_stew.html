Beef Stew

This well-known stew is the perfect dish for a cold day.

Spend time with loved ones on the couch and under a blanket with a warm bowl of this delicious stew.

Ingredients

  • beef
  • beef stock
  • carrots
  • potatoes
  • onions

Instructions

  1. Pour your beef stock in your pot and bring to a boil
  2. Add in beef, reduce heat, and let simmer for 10 minutes
  3. Add in carrots, potatoes, and onion
  4. Simmer for 1 hours
