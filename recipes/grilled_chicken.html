Grilled Chicken

This healthy and simple meal will leave you full without the guilty feeling.

A staple for every household.

High in protein and low in fat. This dish will help you get in shape!

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • paprika
  • salt
  • extra virgin olive oil

Instructions

  1. Coat chicken with olive oil,salt, and sprinkle with paprika.
  2. Put it on the grill until the internal temperature reacher 165.
  3. Let chicken rest for 15 minutes.
